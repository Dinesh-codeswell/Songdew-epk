# Product Strategy: Songdew Artist EPK

**Author:** Gemini CLI Strategy Lead
**Status:** Draft / Demonstration
**Date:** May 27, 2026

---

## 1. Product Vision: The Essence
*Connecting the product to Songdew's broader "Music Business Administration" (MBA) mission.*

**For** Independent musicians and bands
**Who** struggle to maintain a unified, professional digital identity that impresses industry power players
**The Songdew EPK** is a premium, gallery-grade digital press kit
**That** streamlines discovery, evaluation, and booking by industry stakeholders (labels, festivals, brands).
**Unlike** fragmented social media feeds or generic link-in-bio tools,
**Our Product** provides an editorial-style "CV" that integrates directly with Songdew's distribution and monetization suite, turning a profile into a professional business asset.

**Why Now?**
The "Independent Era" of music has led to a saturation of content. Industry curators are overwhelmed. There is a critical need for a "high-signal" tool that filters the noise and presents an artist's career as a compelling, professional story.

---

## 2. Product Insights: The Logic
*Grounded in market reality and Songdew’s unique position.*

### Competitors
*   **Aesthetic Competitors**: Behance (Professional but too design-focused), Adobe Portfolio.
*   **Functional Competitors**: Spotify for Artists (Great data, no storytelling), Linktree (Functional but low-fidelity/slop-prone).
*   **Direct Rivals**: Sonicbids, ReverbNation (Aging platforms with cluttered SaaS aesthetics).

### Market Trends
*   **Direct-to-Industry (D2I)**: Artists are increasingly bypassing traditional gatekeepers; the EPK is their primary negotiation tool.
*   **Visual Storytelling**: High-quality video and photo content are now more influential than audio alone in securing festival slots.

### Customer Insights (Personas)
*   **"The Rising Pro" (Arjun)**: Needs to look established even if they are just starting. Values "Profile Strength" gamification to ensure they haven't missed critical details.
*   **"The Global Scout" (Curator)**: Needs a single-page scrolling view to evaluate an artist on a mobile device in under 60 seconds while backstage or in transit.

---

## 3. Challenges: The Roadblocks
*Anticipating hurdles in technical execution and market adoption.*

*   **Technical**: Ensuring fluid, magazine-style layouts across all device types without sacrificing load speed (under 2s for 4K media assets).
*   **Customer Pain Points**: "Data Entry Fatigue"—artists hate re-uploading music. We must solve this by syncing with Songdew's core distribution database.
*   **GTM Risks**: Breaking through the noise of established "free" link tools. We must prove that a "Premium" EPK leads to higher booking conversion.
*   **Legal**: Protecting artist contact data from scrapers while keeping it accessible for legitimate business enquiries.

---

## 4. Approaches: The Paths
*Our strategic bets to overcome challenges and reach the vision.*

*   **The "Editorial-First" Approach**: We will not build a dashboard; we will build a magazine. Every feature (like the Single-Page View) is designed to make the artist look like a "Brand."
*   **Infrastructure Investment**: Automating image and video optimization so artists can upload raw assets while fans see optimized, fast-loading previews.
*   **The "Done for You" Sync**: Leveraging Songdew's existing data to "pre-fill" the EPK, reducing the time-to-value for our 70k+ existing artists.

**Do's & Don'ts:**
*   **DO**: Enforce a strict, premium design system.
*   **DO**: Prioritize mobile scrolling performance.
*   **DON'T**: Allow custom CSS or un-vetted fonts that could dilute the brand quality.
*   **DON'T**: Turn the EPK into a social network with comments or likes. Keep it a professional tool.

---

## 5. Accountability: The Measure
*How we track success and hold the team to the vision.*

### North Star Metric
*   **Booking-Ready Artist Rate**: % of active users with a >90% Profile Strength score and at least one "Business Enquiry" click.

### Strategic OKRs
*   **Objective**: Become the industry standard for independent music pitches.
    *   **KR1**: 15,000 EPKs created by Songdew Pro members in Q3.
    *   **KR2**: Achieve an average "Time to First Enquiry" of under 30 days for new EPKs.
    *   **KR3**: 98% Lighthouse performance score on all public-facing EPK links.

---

## 6. Roadmap & Phasing

*   **Phase 1 (Shipped)**: Core "Dual-Mode" interface, Single-Page Portfolio, Visibility Toggles, Responsive Adaptation.
*   **Phase 2 (Q3 2026)**: Integration with Songdew Distribution (Auto-sync releases), Advanced Analytics (Who is viewing your EPK?), QR Code generator for live shows.
*   **Phase 3 (Q4 2026)**: "The Pitch" Engine—AI-assisted cover letter generator based on EPK data to help artists apply for Songdew opportunities.

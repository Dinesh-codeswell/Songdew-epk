# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Independent music artists (singers, composers, bands) who need a professional digital identity, and industry stakeholders (labels, festival curators) looking to discover and book them.

## Product Purpose
A premium artist-first digital identity and EPK platform that bridges the gap between a portfolio (Behance) and a music identity (Spotify).

## Brand Personality
- **Premium**: High-end, gallery-like feel.
- **Editorial**: Magazine-style layouts and typography.
- **Modern**: Fluid, interactive, and crisp.

## Anti-references
- **AI Slop**: Generic gradients, overused font families (Inter/Arial), and "placeholder" aesthetics.
- **Cluttered SaaS**: Avoid dense, information-overloaded dashboards.
- **Generic Social Linktrees**: Avoid low-effort, list-only profile styles.

## Design Principles
- **Visual Storytelling**: Every section should feel like a chapter in the artist's career.
- **Content First**: Design serves the media (music, video, photos), not the other way around.
- **Tactile Polish**: High-fidelity hover states and smooth transitions that make the tool feel "alive."

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for metadata.
- Large touch targets for mobile accessibility.
- Screen-reader friendly semantic HTML.

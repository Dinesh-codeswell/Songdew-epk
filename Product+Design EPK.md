# **Songdew Artist Profile Platform — Product Requirements Document (PRD)**

## **Product Vision**

Create a premium artist-first digital identity platform inspired by Behance and Dribbble, but purpose-built for musicians, singers, composers, performers, bands, producers, and music industry professionals.

The platform should function as:

* A professional portfolio for music artists  
* A discovery platform for fans and industry stakeholders  
* A media-ready electronic press kit (EPK)  
* A storytelling platform for artist branding  
* A central identity layer for showcasing music, achievements, live performances, press coverage, visuals, and assets

The experience should feel:

* Premium  
* Modern  
* Editorial  
* Clean  
* Highly visual  
* Minimal but information-rich  
* Industry-grade  
* Creator-focused

The overall visual direction must combine:

* Behance-like portfolio presentation  
* Dribbble-like polished UI  
* Spotify-inspired music visuals  
* Songdew brand aesthetics  
* Editorial storytelling layouts

---

# **Core Product Goals**

## **Primary Goals**

### **1\. Artist Identity**

Allow artists to build a beautiful public-facing digital identity.

### **2\. Discovery**

Enable music fans, labels, event organizers, media, brands, and collaborators to discover artists.

### **3\. Credibility**

Help artists appear professional through:

* Achievements  
* Press mentions  
* Assets  
* Verified links  
* Storytelling  
* Performance history

### **4\. Engagement**

Encourage profile completion using:

* Empty states  
* Profile progress  
* Smart onboarding  
* Guided completion flows  
* CTA-driven UX

### **5\. Conversion**

Drive actions such as:

* Connect  
* Follow  
* Listen  
* Book performances  
* Download EPK  
* Contact management

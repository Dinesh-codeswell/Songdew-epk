Executive Summary
This document outlines the full product strategy, phased execution plan, and launch timeline for the 
Songdew EPK (Electronic Press Kit) Module
. The EPK Module is the first major initiative in a broader platform transformation that positions Songdew as the professional identity layer for India's independent music ecosystem — 
"Songdew for Artists, what LinkedIn is for Professionals."
Core Objective
Deliver a production-ready EPK module within 5.5 weeks that gives independent artists a single, shareable professional profile — and gives industry stakeholders (curators, labels, brands) a purpose-built discovery experience.

1. Problem Statement
Songdew currently presents a single dashboard to two fundamentally different user types — working artists and industry professionals. This creates friction for both.
1.1  Current State Gaps
Artists and listeners/curators share the same dashboard navigation — wrong context for both
The e-Press Kit is a sub-menu item buried inside an artist workstation; industry stakeholders cannot find it without guidance
One URL (songdew.com/profile) tries to serve the working artist AND the curious curator — neither gets an optimal experience

8. Success Metrics — OKRs
Three objectives with measurable Key Results, directly tied to discovery, stakeholder friction, and booking conversion.
North Star Metric: 
% of artists reaching 'Discovery Ready' status within 48 hours of EPK sign-up
O1: Drive Profile Completion
60%+ artists reach 'Discovery Ready' (≥80% profile strength) within 48h of signup
35% of active users reach 100% Profile Strength within 90 days
55% of artists add ≥1 release in first session
O2: Reduce Stakeholder Friction
Avg. time-on-profile (curator segment) ≥ 90 seconds
Curator survey: 50% reduction in vetting time vs. pre-EPK baseline
Bounce rate from profile pages (curator segment) < 40%
O3: Drive Booking Conversions
'Business Enquiry' clicks ≥ 12 per 1,000 profile views
EPK Asset downloads ≥ 8 per 1,000 profile views
Booking enquiries via EPK: +30% month-over-month for 3 consecutive months
